What Is Ascent?

Ascent is a youth-led organization founded by high-school students from Ottawa, Ontario, which aims to expose young students to the expanding fields of the future. We allow students to learn about emerging technologies, (such as AI and augmented reality etc.) explore career options, and network with professionals; as well as take part in interactive workshops, challenges, and much more!! Our aim is to provide the resources and platforms to jump start careers in becoming tomorrow’s intellectual pioneers.

Video

Why Should You Join Ascent?

Have you ever been interested in AI, but don’t know where to start? Despite technology advancing at rates like never before, an overwhelming majority of youth are never provided the opportunities to educate themselves on the things that will propel the future. Ascent exists to solve this problem, and bridge the gap between emerging fields and the youth that must follow them.
